Originally people were good and wise, but we have changed from the beginning of time.

We are not basically good

We do not have an inherent wisdom

However, if we see ourselves as no self -- a consciousness that is without birth or death -- it makes sense.

This basic goodness and wisdom is actually transcendent, God

We must not mistake ourselves for Him, but allow the distinction to continue

Fear of death is not overcome by denying birth

Fear of death is not overcome by denying self-existence

Fear of death is not overcome by denying our flawed nature and wisdom
